His Life

William was born on Febuary 8th, 1820. He first saw combat at the Battle of First Manassas, and impressed his leaders. He was promoted to Brigadeer General.

Background Info

At the Battle of Shiloh, Sherman was overrun by Confederates, but was still promoted to Major General on May 1, 1862. By 1864, Sherman became convinced that the Union’s preservation was dependent not only by defeating the Rebels on the battlefield but also by destroying the Confederacy's will to wage war.
